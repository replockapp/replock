Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RepLock ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application.

By using RepLock, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, home address, or phone number.

However, certain information may be collected to operate the app and improve its performance. This may include:

  • App usage data
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Device information, such as device model, iOS version, and language settings
  • Subscription and paywall interaction data
  • Onboarding preferences and in-app settings, such as:
  • selected challenge level
  • body area preferences
  • physical limitations
  • workout/session preferences
  • lock schedule settings

If you choose to enable Screen Time / Family Controls features, RepLock may also store and use information necessary to operate those features, such as selected apps to restrict and schedule settings. This information is used only to provide the blocking and habit-flow functionality of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information collected through the app to:

  • operate and maintain RepLock
  • deliver the app’s habit-building, session, and scheduling features
  • monitor app performance and stability
  • diagnose bugs and crashes
  • understand general feature usage and product behavior
  • improve the subscription and paywall experience
  • improve the overall user experience

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your information for third-party advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

RepLock uses the following third-party services:

  • Superwall – to present paywalls and manage subscription-related experiences
  • PostHog – to analyze product usage, feature interactions, and app performance

These providers may process limited technical and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We use these services to operate and improve RepLock, not to sell your data.

4. Screen Time / App Restriction Features

RepLock may request access to Apple’s Screen Time / Family Controls features in order to help users restrict access to selected apps during self-defined focus or discipline periods.

If you grant this permission, RepLock uses that access only to:

  • apply user-configured app restriction settings
  • manage lock schedules you create
  • support the app’s habit and session flow

RepLock does not use Screen Time permissions for advertising, tracking, or surveillance purposes.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Some data used by RepLock may be stored locally on your device, such as onboarding selections, session history, schedule preferences, and app settings.

Analytics and diagnostics data collected through third-party services may be retained according to those providers’ retention settings and policies.

6. Your Choices

Because RepLock does not currently require user accounts, you may limit or reset certain app data by:

  • deleting the app from your device
  • resetting app data from within the app, if available
  • disabling permissions such as Screen Time / Family Controls in your device settings

If you would like to request deletion of analytics-related data associated with your device, you may contact us at:

replockapp@gmail.com

We will review and process reasonable requests in accordance with applicable law and the capabilities of our service providers.

7. Children’s Privacy

RepLock is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at replockapp@gmail.com, and we will take appropriate steps to review and delete such information if applicable.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

Your continued use of RepLock after any update becomes effective means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how RepLock handles data, please contact us at:

replockapp@gmail.com
